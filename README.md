# BlueMart Omni-Channel Sales & Inventory Insights

BlueMart Retail LLC is a mid-sized UAE retail and e-commerce chain established in 2018, operating both online (ShopBlueMart.ae) and 25 physical outlets across Dubai, Sharjah, and Abu Dhabi. The firm specializes in consumer goods and personal care products. Facing post-pandemic shifts in demand, BlueMart’s management wants a unified analytics system that tracks KPIs across both retail stores and the e-commerce platform to optimize inventory, marketing spend, and profitability.

Stakeholder : Mr. Adil Khan – Head of Sales & Digital Transformation at BlueMart. 

Objectives: 

• Build an integrated dashboard linking retail and e-commerce KPIs. 

• Measure sales performance, customer retention, marketing ROI, and inventory turnover. 

• Detect regional demand trends and seasonality (Ramadan, Eid, tourist months) in UAE.
