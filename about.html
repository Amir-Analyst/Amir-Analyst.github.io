About Me

I'm Amir Khan — a business analyst transitioning from pharmacy operations to AI-driven business analytics and strategy. My 10+ years of experience across pharmaceutical sales, purchasing, and inventory management in the UAE and India shaped my belief that operational challenges are ultimately data problems waiting for intelligent solutions.

My journey began as a Medical Representative in India, learning how markets, people, and business performance interconnect through data and human behavior. Later in the UAE, I spent over five years in pharmacy purchasing and inventory management, managing vendor relations, procurement strategies, and supply chain operations. These experiences taught me how data, systems, and decisions come together to drive performance.

I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor of Business Administration at Manipal University Jaipur and plan to continue with an MBA in AI-Driven Business Strategy. I’ve also completed certifications in Google Advanced Data Analytics, IBM AI Engineering, and IBM Generative AI Engineering, helping me combine business logic with analytical intelligence.

Current Focus

Developing a self-initiated analytics project, BlueMart Retail Analytics — a simulated omnichannel retail business built on a synthetic dataset. The focus is on exploratory data analysis, generating actionable insights, and providing recommendations to improve sales forecasting, inventory optimization, and channel efficiency.

My Philosophy

Analytics is not just about models — it’s about translating business realities into data narratives that guide smarter, faster decisions. My approach focuses on three principles:

Clarity

Insights must be presented clearly — so decision-makers act confidently.

Impact

Every analytical effort should lead to measurable business improvement.

Scalability

Intelligent systems must evolve with business needs.

"Transforming operations into intelligence — one dataset, one insight, one decision at a time."
