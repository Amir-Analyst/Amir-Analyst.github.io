About Me

My journey from pharmacy to business operations and analytics represents more than a career transition—it's a transformation driven by a fundamental belief: operational inefficiency can be transformed into intelligence.

With 10+ years of experience in pharmaceutical operations, purchasing, and warehouse management across UAE and India, I've witnessed firsthand how data silos, manual processes, and fragmented systems create bottlenecks that impact operational efficiency. This experience sparked my curiosity about how technology and analytics could bridge these gaps.

Today, as a Procurement and Inventory Management specialist at Docib Healthcare LLC, I manage procurement and inventory operations across 36 outlets, collaborating with key vendors to maintain optimal stock levels. My approach combines deep analytical thinking with practical business acumen, leveraging data-driven insights to enhance procurement efficiency and improve overall inventory management processes.

Currently pursuing my Bachelor of Business Administration while expanding expertise in Data Analytics and AI, I'm focused on driving measurable business impact through forecasting and process automation. I'm skilled in supply chain analytics, ERP-driven procurement (Microsoft Dynamics 365), and data-based vendor performance evaluation.
