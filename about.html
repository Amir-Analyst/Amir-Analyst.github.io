Learning Journey

I'm Amir Khan — a business analyst transitioning from pharmacy operations to AI-driven business analytics and strategy. My 10+ years of cross-domain experience across pharmaceutical sales, purchasing, and inventory management in the UAE and India shaped my belief that operational challenges are ultimately data problems waiting for intelligent solutions.

I began as a Medical Representative in India, learning how markets, people, and business performance interconnect through data and human behavior. Later, in the UAE, I managed pharmacy purchasing and inventory, vendor relations, procurement strategies, and supply chain operations — understanding how data and decisions converge to drive business outcomes.

I'm pursuing a BBA and plan an MBA in AI-Driven Business Strategy. I’ve also completed certifications in Google Advanced Data Analytics, IBM AI Engineering, and IBM Generative AI Engineering, combining business logic with analytical intelligence.

Current Focus

Developing BlueMart Retail Analytics — a synthetic omnichannel retail project exploring sales forecasting, inventory optimization, and channel efficiency. This initiative demonstrates applying AI and analytics to real-world business problems through structured, insight-driven storytelling.

Philosophy

Analytics is not just building models — it’s translating business realities into data narratives that drive smarter, faster decisions. My approach is guided by three principles:

Clarity

Insights must be presented clearly — so decision-makers can act confidently, not cautiously.

Impact

Every analytical effort should lead to measurable business improvement or operational intelligence.

Scalability

Intelligent systems must be adaptable — capable of learning and evolving with business needs.

"Transforming operations into intelligence — one dataset, one insight, one decision at a time."
