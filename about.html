About Me

My journey from pharmacy to analytics represents more than a career transition—it's a transformation driven by a fundamental belief: operational inefficiency can be transformed into intelligence.

As a pharmacist, I witnessed firsthand how data silos, manual processes, and fragmented systems created bottlenecks that impacted patient care and operational efficiency. This experience sparked my curiosity about how technology and analytics could bridge these gaps.

Today, as an AI-Driven Business Analyst, I specialize in building intelligent systems that merge analytics, automation, and behavioral insights. My approach combines deep analytical thinking with practical business acumen, enabling organizations to make data-driven decisions that scale.

What I Do

  • ▹ Design and implement predictive analytics models that forecast demand, optimize inventory, and reduce operational costs
  • ▹ Develop automated business intelligence dashboards that transform raw data into actionable insights
  • ▹ Build machine learning solutions that enhance decision-making processes and improve business outcomes
  • ▹ Collaborate with cross-functional teams to translate complex data into strategic business recommendations

My Philosophy

I believe that the best analytics solutions are those that are both technically sound and intuitively usable. Every project I undertake is guided by three principles:

Clarity

Complex data should be presented in a way that anyone can understand and act upon.

Impact

Every solution should deliver measurable business value and drive meaningful change.

Scalability

Systems must be designed to grow and adapt as business needs evolve.
