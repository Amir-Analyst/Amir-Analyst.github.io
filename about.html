About Me

I’m Amir Khan — a Data Analyst and AI-driven Business Strategist with 10.5+ years of experience across retail, pharmacy, purchasing, and supply chain operations in the UAE and India. My unique edge is combining real-world operational experience with analytics, automation, and AI to solve business challenges with clarity and intelligence.

I started my career in India as a Medical Representative, where I learned how markets, customer behavior, and product performance shape business outcomes. After moving to the UAE, I spent five years managing pharmacy purchasing, multi-outlet inventory, forecasting, and supplier coordination. Working inside operational bottlenecks taught me a key insight: most operational problems are actually data problems — and they require analytical systems, not manual fixes.

Today, I specialize in building AI-ready analytics systems — transforming raw ERP data (e.g., Dynamics 365) into insights, forecasts, dashboards, and automation pipelines. I combine business logic with technical skills through a growing stack that includes Python, SQL, Streamlit, Power BI, machine learning, workflow automation, and product-oriented analytics design.

Current Focus

I’m building two full-scale portfolio systems that reflect real business scenarios:

BlueMart Retail Analytics — a simulated omni-channel retail product using a realistic synthetic dataset (2–5M annual transactions, 50+ stores, 5K–10K SKUs). It focuses on EDA, forecasting, inventory optimization, customer insights, promotions analysis, and a Streamlit multi-page dashboard powered by Plotly and dynamic KPIs.

HomeVista — a UAE rental analytics system for price prediction, market segmentation, and scenario modeling.

My Philosophy

Analytics is most powerful when it feels like a product — clear, actionable, and scalable. My decision-making principles are:

Clarity

Insights should be simple, intuitive, and decision-ready.

Impact

Analytics must drive measurable improvements, not just reports.

Scalability

Systems should evolve with business growth — built with automation and longevity in mind.
