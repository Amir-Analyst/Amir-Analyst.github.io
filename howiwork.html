My Methodology

Turning Chaos into Clarity with AI-Augmented Analytics

I don't just write code — I orchestrate solutions. By combining operational intuition with AI-driven engineering, I build scalable analytics systems faster and more reliably than traditional methods.

The Operational Analytics Framework

1

Operational Discovery

I go to the floor first. Before touching data, I understand the physical process — how inventory moves, how suppliers communicate, where the bottlenecks live. Data without context is just noise.

2

AI-Augmented Engineering

I architect, AI builds. I define the logic and business rules, then leverage AI agents to generate robust Python/SQL pipelines. This allows me to be tool-agnostic and focus on solving the business problem, not just syntax.

3

Analytical Modeling

Finding the signal. I move beyond "what happened" to "why" and "what's next". Using Python libraries (Pandas, NumPy), I structure messy operational data into clean, decision-ready insights.

4

Actionable Delivery

Dashboards that get used. I design for decision-makers, not data scientists. Whether it's a Streamlit app or an automated report, the output is always clear, visual, and directly tied to business action.

My Tech Philosophy

Tool Agnostic

I don't get hung up on syntax. Whether a project needs SQL, Python, or a new framework, I use AI tools to adapt instantly. My expertise is in solving the problem, not memorizing the dictionary.

Speed to Value

Business moves fast. I prefer rapid prototyping — building a working MVP (Minimum Viable Product) to get immediate feedback, then iterating based on real usage.
